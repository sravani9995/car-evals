ABOUT CARS EVALS

A used car or a pre-owned car as it is called is a car which is already used by one or multiple users for a certain time period. Banks or finance agencies offer loan for such pre-owned cars . Since cars are depreciating assets and tend to lose their value with every passing year the loan amount for such cars are comparatively low than the loan amount offered for a new car. This is a special type of car loan where the banks provide a loan to the borrowers against their car to meet their financial requirements. Borrowers who are in need of money benefit from this type of car loan.

Enquiry with a banker

In the very first step, the applicants need to fill up an applicationform of their desired bank with all the vital particulars such as their personal details and otherrequired credentials.

Field Investigation

After the submission of the application, the borrowers are required to upload all the documents mandated by the banks.

Loan approved

Once the banker is satisfied with the veracity of your documents, the loan is approved. The banker then disburses the amount.
